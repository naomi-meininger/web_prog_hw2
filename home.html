Welcome to Home for Hounds

Mission Statement:

Our mission is to provide loving homes for abandoned
dogs by matching them with responsible and compassionate
owners through a thorough foster and/or adoption process,
promoting responsible pet ownership and supporting our
adopters for the lifetime of the dog.
